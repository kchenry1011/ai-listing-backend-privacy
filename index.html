Privacy Policy

AI Title & Description Generator Chrome Extension

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Title & Description Generator ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Password: Stored securely using industry-standard encryption (bcrypt hashing)
  • API Usage Data: We track the number of API calls made to manage your account limits

Product Images

  • Images You Upload: Product images are temporarily processed through OpenAI's API to generate listing content
  • Image Storage: We do not permanently store your product images on our servers
  • Third-Party Processing: Images are sent to OpenAI's API for AI analysis and are subject to OpenAI's privacy policy

Technical Information

  • Authentication Tokens: JWT tokens are stored locally in your browser for session management
  • Browser Storage: The extension uses Chrome's local storage to maintain your login session

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and maintain your user account
  • Service Delivery: To provide AI-powered listing generation services
  • Usage Tracking: To monitor API usage and enforce account limits
  • Communication: To respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Service Improvement: To improve our AI models and service quality

Data Storage and Security

  • Secure Storage: User credentials are stored in a secure PostgreSQL database with encrypted passwords
  • Local Storage: Authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to third parties
  • HTTPS: All data transmission between the extension and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • No Image Storage: Product images are not permanently stored after processing

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: Product images are sent to OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision API for analysis. OpenAI's data usage is governed by their Privacy Policy
  • Render: Our backend servers are hosted on Render for reliable service delivery

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared:

  • With OpenAI for the purpose of AI image analysis (as described above)
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your explicit consent

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured format

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Product Images: Not retained after processing is complete
  • Usage Data: Retained for billing and service improvement purposes
  • Deleted Accounts: All associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies. The extension only stores authentication tokens locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Permissions Explanation

The extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to interact with the currently active eBay listing page
  • scripting: Required to populate eBay's listing form fields with generated content
  • storage: Used to store authentication tokens locally in your browser
  • host_permissions (*.ebay.com): Limits the extension to work only on eBay.com domains
